**Layoff Data Analysis**

This repository contains a dataset and analysis on layoff trends across various industries, companies, and countries. The dataset includes information about companies, their locations, industries, the number and percentage of employees laid off, and the funds raised by each company.

**Dataset Description**

The dataset consists of the following columns:

Company: The name of the company affected by layoffs.
Location: The geographic location of the company.
Industry: The sector to which the company belongs (e.g., Healthcare, Marketing, Crypto).
Total Laid Off: The total number of employees laid off.
Percentage Laid Off: The percentage of the workforce laid off.
Date: The date when the layoffs were announced.
Stage: The funding stage of the company (e.g., Series A, Series B, Post-IPO).
Country: The country where the company is based.
Funds Raised (Millions): The amount of funding raised by the company (in millions of USD).
